What is a
dirty flag?

It's when you're losing at chess, or in a drawn position... but there's a glimmer of hope. You're ahead of your opponent on the clock. You play fast, so fast your enemy can't keep up. They run out of time. The game's over. You win, even though you were down a pawn, a piece, or even a queen. That rush of adrenaline, that fountain of salt from your opponent: That's a dirty flag.
